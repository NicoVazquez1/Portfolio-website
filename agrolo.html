Agrolo - Optimizing the Fruit Harvesting Process

Note: Normally, I program in English, but I also wanted to teach others who don't speak English, which is why I chose the Spanish language for this project.

Let's talk about something useful. This project aims to streamline the process of recording fruit weight, revenue generated, worker payments, tray quantity, client information, and delivery type in a blueberry field. I myself worked in such a field a few years ago and witnessed firsthand how slow the process was and how it could be improved with this app. So, I proposed the idea to the field owner, and he liked it.
